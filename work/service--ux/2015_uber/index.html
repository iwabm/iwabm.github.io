UBER UX RESEARCH AND DESIGN

UX/UI Design | 2015, Academic Work

Design Question: How can we improve the experience of Uber drivers?

Joined 5 weeks MFA Interaction Design Program of User Research Methods in SVA, US. Woriking in teams, we’ve joined a real project with Uber and proposed UX to solve current issues. I’ve learned the fundamentals of user research techniques and the sense of UX in collaboration with global students.
Teacher: Jodi Leo, VP of Product Design at CapTap

OUR APPROACH: Experience Design based on Observing

Learned methodologies and techniques of the research in a lecture (Week 1). Interviewed an executive of Uber and defined the goal of this project; “perfecting the pickup“ (Week 2). Observed the service of Uber. we went to JFK airport and used the service actually. We interviewed the driver and summarized findings using AEIOU worksheet (Week 3). Extracted insights of the research as the class. We’ve defined current issues of the service and our team tackled with a language and communication probrem between the driver and the rider (Week 4).

Design Process & My Contribution

Phase: Concept

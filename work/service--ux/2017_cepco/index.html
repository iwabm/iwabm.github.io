C-EP CONSUMER SERVICES

Service Design | 2017, Professiona Work

WHY WE DID: Which direction should the electric power company aim for in the 21st century?

In 2016, full-scale deregulation of the electricity industry in Japan has begun, and a lot of new and emerging companies have entered the electricity market. Chubu Electric Power Company (C-EPCO), a Japanese electricity utility provider for the mid-Chubu region of Honshu island, Japan, must shift to a new paradigm of customer service in order to improve its retention capability.

WHAT WE DID: Design comprehensive service ecosystem that tightens family bonds of their customers

We have been cultivating a new paradigm of customer service ecosystem that tightens family bonds of their customers using iPhone, Apple Watch and IoT Robot.

We are developing an iOS app with a robot toy that can communicate with children. By recording their daily responses and sharing these with the children’s parents and grandparents, the family members are able to understand the children’s hidden preferences and feel as if they are together, even if they live far apart.

As a project manager and professional UX designer, I contributed to the project from the concept phase to the agile development phase. In terms of technology, customer experience and sustainable business model, we have been designing entire new business ecosystem of the client.

Make initial creative concept focusing on customer experience

At the beginning, in the concept phase, we planned the IBM Design Thinking workshop with clients, created the initial concept for a new service.

From these bold ideas, we prioritized the concept by repeated 10 interviews and finally created the first user scenarios and static mockup screens.

Fix requirements of business model, user experience and tech specs

In the following lean startup phase, we specified and verified business opportunities and appropriate user experiences. Collaborating with business strategists and engineers, we created the detailed service ecosystem in terms of business, technology and design.

We performed 30 interviews and improved our product iteratively. Using InVision, we performed interviews with a moving prototype mockup. Finally, we fixed requirements of comprehensive services and proceeded to the following development phase.

Finalize UX/UI in agile enginnering phase

In the subsequent development phase, I have been working on continuously improving the quality of the product through a process of iterative prototyping and user evaluation. I have the certification of both a practitioner of IBM Design Thinking and a Scrum Master, so I can work consistently end to end from the concept creation to implementation phases as a professional user experience designer.

Service Design is Co-creation

Product design is a process of exploring an appropriate ecosystem comprising everyone, that is created by everyone, and is provided for everyone.

My guiding principle is co-creation with clients, users, stakeholders, and experts to advance the journey between ideal concept and viable function; innovation and cliché; and provider’s aspiration and the customer’s need.

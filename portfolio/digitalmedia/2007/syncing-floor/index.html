Syncing Floor

2007, Interactive Installation
Exhibited the interactive installation which connects 2 distant exhibition sites.

Concept

Visitors can feel the bustle of the exhibition site inconspicuously.

Challenge

I decided to join a media art exhibition in the Univ. of Tokyo. This exhibition has 2 distant sites so I utilized this characteristic and I planned to visualize an atmosphere of the sites.

Approach

According to the number of visitors, towns in the display will grow. Growing speed is different according to the flow of visitors and buildings or characters in the display are different according to the time of visitors’ coming. Syncing Floor is a installation art which visualizes ‘‘bustle’’ of distant places.

Result

This installation art was exhibited at iii Exhibition 7, and connected 2 distant exhibition sites by visualising ‘‘bustle’’ of 2 distant exhibition sites(2nd floor and 9th floor).

Exhibitions

iii exhibition 7, The University of Tokyo, Japan, 2007.

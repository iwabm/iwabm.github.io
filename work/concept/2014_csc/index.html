IBM CSC

2014, Pro Bono Consulting | Professional Work

Design Question:

How do we establish a preferable future vision on a social level?

IBM CSC(Corporate Service Corp) is one-month pro bono consulting program for developing countries addressing Energy, Economic Development, Transportation, Education, and Healthcare. I participated in the IBM pro bono Corporate Service Corps, addressing social issues in developing countries. I was one of a cross-border global IBM team in Mexico that sought to tackle the problems of gender violence by collaborating with the Mexico State Public Administration.

Ethnographic Approach

Using ethnographic approach, we’ve done a diagnosis of existing services and proposed a business strategy of IAPEM. Although the final output was minimal, it provided a valuable opportunity for observation in a foreign environment and to empathize with other people’s experiences.

Design Process & My Contribution

Data Research

Field Observing & Business Goal Setting

Insight Analizing & Issue Defining

Solution Exploring & Disussing

Prioritize Solutions & Strategy Planning

Protect Victims, Prevent Crimes

Victims can know what they should do by themselves when damaged and get support immediately.

Through investigating their services and environments, my team proposed technological solutions like a GPS monitoring bracelet device that could help to prevent gender violence and advocate citizen involvement in the community. The Administration office continued to work on the implementation of our solutions.

My Continuing Inquiry

I continued to question whether a top-down approach was the appropriate process.
Through collaborating with the government, this valuable volunteer experience taught me the importance of a long-term vision and keen insights to make a social impact. However, I continued to question whether a top-down approach was the appropriate process. I thus became keen to explore a co-creation process at the community and social level. Proactive critical discussions needed to take place in collaboration not only with the government but also with citizens.

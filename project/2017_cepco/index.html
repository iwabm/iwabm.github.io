Background

After the deregulation, which direction should the electric power company aim for?

In 2016, full-scale deregulation of the electricity industry in Japan has begun, and a lot of new and emerging companies have entered the electricity market. Chubu Electric Power Company (C-EPCO), one of Japanese electricity utility providers, must shift to a new paradigm of customer service in order to improve its retention capability.

Problem

How might we create tight family bonds in recent Japanese families?

This project focus on problems in the family because electric power company’s mission is supporting lives of families.

In this project, we focus on a dual-income working mother. According to many interviews with working mother, we found how hard working parents take care of little children. When a working mother comes back to the home with her child, she must do many houseworks such as cooking, washing clothes and so on. Current mothers often leave her child watching youtube or playing games and communications between parents and children is getting less. Some young mothers lose a way how to take care of children. This is one of the current social problems of Japan.

Concept

Create the ecosystem that tightens cusutomers’ family bonds to improve the retention rate

Our team cultivated a new paradigm of customer service ecosystem that tightens family bonds of their customers using iPhone, Apple Watch and IoT Robot at a cost of $100M over the course of a year. Finally my team designed and finalized 94 screens and interactions in the development phase.

We developed an iOS app with a robot toy that can communicate with children. By recording their daily responses and sharing these with the children’s parents and grandparents, the family members are able to understand the children’s hidden preferences and feel as if they are together, even if they live far apart.

Result

Launch in Public in Fall 2018

We performed a qualitative and quantitative user evaluation and got a high score. Therefore, the client decide to launch this App in the next year cycle. Currently, the client continues to develop Android version and they are planning to release this product in Fall 2018.

Design Approach

Design Thinking + Lean Startup + Agile Development

Using design thinking, while focusing on customer-oriented thinking, we define the strong concept eventually to be released as a service running in the society. With the concept of lean startup, we verify and grow the service concept as a feasible business model. Finally we implement the business ecosystem designed with such a way of thinking to the world with agile development.

My Role

Project Management and UX/UI Design

As a project manager and professional UX designer, I contributed to the project from the concept phase to the agile development phase. In terms of technology, customer experience and sustainable business model, my team designed entire new business ecosystem of the client.

In the concept phase, I planned a Design Thinking workshop with clients and created the initial concept of the service, prior to narrowing this down to a series of actionable steps in consideration of the concept’s implementation. Additionally, in the subsequent development phase, I have worked on continuously improving the quality of the product through a process of iterative prototyping and user evaluation. I am certified as both a practitioner of IBM Design Thinking and a Scrum Master, which means I can work consistently end to end, from the concept creation to the implementation phases, as a professional user experience designer.

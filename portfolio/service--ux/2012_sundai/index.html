Sundai

2012, Corporate Websiteg
Professional Work
Redesigned comprehensive online user experience for SUNDAI, one of the largest preparatory school in Japan.

concept: Students can get helpful personalized information and keep motivation throughout end-to-end college entrance examination process. challenge: Clients’ website was obsoleted and clients wanted to grow their core customers using current web technologies and usability disciplines. Of course they’d like to reach these objectives in an efficient operation. approach: The project has reformed an overall user experience of “SUNDAI” website and delivered the new visual design for PCs and smartphones. This includes web strategy consulting, front-end design and development, workflow design, existing data transition, CMS(Contents Management System) development and maintenance. Adapting IBM UCD(User Centered Design) approach, we’ve defined contents acoording to user’s insights. And also we’ve implemented CMS and realized some interactive functions for users such as content personalization. This function is the first case in Japanese preparatory school. result: In the result, Page View of the website has increased and the client’s work efficiency has also increased (23% increase of PV, 136% increase of unique visitors, 1min22s increase of visit duration). We’ve got great client’s satisfaction. workcat: Professional Work pjtype: Service Design phase: Concept, Design, Build, Run period: 09/2011 - 09/2012 role: UI/UX Design Practitioner, Data Transition Lead method: User-Centered Design, Waterfall engineering device: PC, Mobile team: PM, Designers(Me), CMS Engineers, Infra specialists output: Competitive Analysis Chart, Persona, Site Concept, Customer Journey Map, Site Structure, Wireframes, Mockup Screens, To-Be Business Model release: 09/2012 - until now tech: CMS(Oracle Fatwire)

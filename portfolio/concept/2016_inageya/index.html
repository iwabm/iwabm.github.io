INAGEYA

2016, Strategic Concept | Professional Work

Design Question:

What is “beyond” supermarket?

In a situation that society and family structures are changing, a role of supermarket also needs to change in Japan. Clients were struggling for planning a strong concept of a next-generation supermarket in 2020 with consideration for a sustainable ecosystem and of course customer-first.

Design Thinking: Dive into “the customer”

Using IBM Design Thinking, we supported to ideate a brand-new future user experience and defined the concept of next-gen supermarket only in 2 days intensive design camp. We started to define personas to understand our customers and then explored concrete ideas and abstract concepts; diverged and converged iteratively.

Next-Generation Supermarket in 2020

Customers and their families can turn their household worries into fun and create their sustainable local communities together.

As IBM Design Thinking instructor, I have managed and facilitated the discussion of entire workshop process with collaborating with designers and retail-industry experts in IBM. We finally reached integrated one concept of the next-gen supermarket and achieved a consensus beyond all departments of the client. In the last activity, we defined next To-Dos, and the client could go forward to a next step.

HUMAN EXPERIENCE FOR TRANSHUMAN

XHuman is an educational headset for transhumans who inplants superintelligence into their left brain which works for language, memory, calculation. This gadget stimulates your brain electrically and temporarily stops the neurotransmission between the brain chip and the brain. This feature enables transhumans to think as “Humans”.

Aug, 2018

Concept

XHuman: Educational Headset for transhumans to Experience “Human” in 2075

Project Information

  • Phase / Period
    • Concept, Design, Build
    • Period* 10⁄2018 (2 weeks)
  • Team / Role
    • Researcher(Me), Professor
  • Method
    • Future Cone, Future’s Wheel, Timeline, Persona, Empathy Map, Storyboard, technological research

  • Output* Physical prototype, User’s Manual

Objective

How can Humans and Transhumans coexist towards a sustainable society in 2075?

In 2075, this is the first generation that humans and transhumans are coexisting in the society. There is a new social problem that human beings and transhumans can not understand each other. Transhuman can not understand why humans are so inefficient. Humans were also afraid of transhuman beyond their abilities and did not know how to work or live together.

Possible Timeline

In 2045, AI finally exceeded the intelligence of all human beings.

In 2060, superintelligence is embedded in the human brain for the first time and Human consciousness uploaded on the internet.

Now in 2075, 11% of parents choose to implant AI to their newborn babies. They are the first generation of transhuman society.

Recently transhuman-only school is established because their superintelligence and don’t need “normal” human’s curriculum anymore.

Design Approach

Future speculation using wearable technology

Create a speculative body technology that interacts with our sensory input or output. This device could enhance the way we see, limit our ability to smell or hear…This device could be useful for a future version of our world, or another world/society from our imagination.

Make a prototype of an object, along with user manual that illustrates how it works and how one would use it.

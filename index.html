About Me

Masaki is an award-winning interaction and service designer experienced in a wide array of projects from tangible products to intangible services.

Currently, he is a MFA candidate of the Design + Technology program at Parsons School of Design to explore speculative and future-oriented design methodologies.

Previously he worked at IBM Design and has 10 years of professional UX/UI design experience in the Human Centered Design discipline.

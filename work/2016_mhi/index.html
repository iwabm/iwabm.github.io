IBM Watson cognitive enterprise app

Enterprise Application Design | 2016, Professional Work

Objective

How can MHI engineers make the RFP inspection process more effectively?

RFP inspection is important work for Mitsubishi Heavy Industries, Ltd. (MHI) engineers to estimate risks in advance and deliver the project in a healthy way. However, it needs much knowledge and takes long time to do it accurately under the pressure of a deadline.

Concept

Cognitive Advisor on worker’s task

MHI is looking for developing a cognitive application platform like ‘‘Artificial Advisor’’ that will issues that engineers face during the inspection. Those issues range from regulation awareness, gaining insights from massive quantities of data and capturing knowledge from an aging talent pool.

Therefore, required functions are the following.

  • Store all texts and charts of RFP from MHI’s clients
  • Extract sentences and items which have potential risks by comparing with past projects
  • Show related international regulations and past case studies
  • Support making a proposal

We took the first step!

We delivered an initial phase of a cognitive Watson Advisor solution using agile engineering approach. MHI engineers can inspect and take action for risks of their projects in advance without long industry experience. They reduced the time taken by the worker’s task by 30% as a result.

Clients needs ‘‘innovative and unique UI’’ to show brand-new experience internally, so we experimented various UIs with real users and applied the unique interaction inspired by the cube. We released the first product on time and proceed to the next iterative development. The project team will experiment watson’s possibilities and more supportive functions.

Human-Centered Design according to UX 5-Planes Model

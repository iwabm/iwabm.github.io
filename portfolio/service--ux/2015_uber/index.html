Uber

2015, iOS App Concept
Academic Work
Designed new feature for mobile app of UBER in the 5-weeks Summer MFA Interaction Design Program (User Research Methods course) in School of Visual Arts, US.

concept: A driver and a rider can meet 100% accuracy without inconvenience using a translating function. challenge: Joined 5 weeks MFA Interaction Design Program of User Research Methods in SVA, US. Woriking in teams, we’ve joined a real project with Uber and proposed UX to solve current issues. I’ve learned the fundamentals of user research techniques and the sense of UX in collaboration with global students. The program was following. approach: Learned methodologies and techniques of the research in a lecture (Week 1). Interviewed an executive of Uber and defined the goal of this project; “perfecting the pickup“ (Week 2). Observed the service of Uber. we went to JFK airport and used the service actually. We interviewed the driver and summarized findings using AEIOU worksheet (Week 3). Extracted insights of the research as the class. We’ve defined current issues of the service and our team tackled with a language and communication probrem between the driver and the rider (Week 4). result: Gave the final presentation to the class. We proposed an automatic translation function and overall UX and showed the possibility of investing (Week 5). workcat: Academic Work pjtype: UX Design phase: Concept period: 7⁄2015 - 08/2015 role: UI/UX Design Practitioner method: User-Centered Design device: Mobile team: Design practitioners which had various backgrounds output: Field Research Analysis Sheet, Persona, Customer Journey Map, Mockup Screens release:

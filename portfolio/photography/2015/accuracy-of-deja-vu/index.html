Accuracy of Deja-vu

2015, Photography

Concept / Description

Sometimes I feel as if I've seen a place that I have visited before. When I had this feeling in Tokyo and Osaka, which are representative cities of the East and West of Japan, I tried to find what made me feel that way. This is my way to test my feeling of deja vu and also of seeking out something unique in these cities.

MTA Metrocard Redesign

UX/UI Design | 2018, Private Work

*This project is still ongoing

Objective

How might we make NYC’s subway riding experience more comfortable?

The NYC metrocard system has remained unchanged for decades and users of NYC subway sometimes have uncomfortable experiences especially the entrance of subway; the lost time of waiting in line to buy a metrocard, touching a dirty machine and so on. User-centered service and experience is needed for users to get access easily to the metro, on time, without having a bad experience.

Concept

Gateless Entry

Subway users can enter the platform without having physical metrocard.

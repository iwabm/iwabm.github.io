Daifuku

2015, Corporate Website | Professional Work

Service Concept

Customers can know DAIFUKU’s strength intuitively and get same services from anywhere in the world.

Challenge

Clients thought their website was becoming obsolete and wanted to enhance their brand using current web technologies and user-centered disciplines. And Daifuku has 20 branches in the world, so they wanted to provide global integrated contents and operations.

Approach

Based on a service design approach, we designed and proposed from a business organization to UX / UI and an infrastructure. The project has defined the concept of the overall user experience of global “DAIFUKU” website and prototyped the new visual design for PCs and smartphones.

Result

I have worked as a UI/UX Leader and defined the concept of whole online experience of the website. In collaboration with a business consultant and an Infrastructure technologist, we’ve determined a site structure and contents according to personas and then delivered visual and interaction mockups. Finally, we could proceed to the next development phase.

Design Process & My Contribution

Understand

We researched the current environment of the website from design and business side and agreed with business objectives and goals.

Define

As a UX/UI design lead, I determined the site concept through persona and user scenario analyzing.

Explore

With clients, we discussed contents, wireframes, and the site structure iteratively.

Create

Finally, we proposed the brand-new website UI design in collaboration with a graphic designer.

Service Ecosystem Design

We also collaborated with a business consultant and an IT architect and developed a global organization, an operation flow, and an infrastructure/backend system. We integrated business, technology, and design perspectives and provided overall service ecosystem comprehensively.

DAIFUKU EXPERIENCE

UX/UI Design | 2015, Professiona Work

Objective

How does DAIFUKU build a globally unified brand?

Clients thought their website was becoming obsolete and wanted to enhance their brand using current web technologies and user-centered disciplines. And Daifuku has 20 branches in the world, so they wanted to provide global integrated contents and operations.

Concept

Redesign DAIFUKU corporate website and operations

From the perspective of Business, Design and Technology, we designed and proposed from a business organization to UX / UI and an infrastructure. The project has defined the concept of the overall user experience of global “DAIFUKU” website and prototyped the new visual design for PCs and smartphones.

Design Appproach

Human-Centered Design according to UX 5-Planes Model

Using UX 5-Planes Model, we redesigned comprehensive DAIFUKU experience.

My Role

Lead UX/UI design

I have worked as a UI/UX Leader and defined the concept of whole online experience of the website. In collaboration with a business consultant and an Infrastructure technologist, we’ve determined a site structure and contents according to personas and then delivered visual and interaction mockups. Finally, we could proceed to the next development phase.

Result

Globally Unified Corporate Website

Finally, we opened the new corporate website. Customers can know DAIFUKU’s strength intuitively and get same services from anywhere in the world.

http://www.daifuku.do.jp/

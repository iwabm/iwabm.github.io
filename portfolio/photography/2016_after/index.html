PHOTOGRAPHY 100Y AFTER

2016, Photography | Personal Work

Personal Question:

What kind of picture do we take 100 years later?

This is a private project that comes from questions.
This is a speculative and experimental imagination for our future 22nd century.

virtual forest

This is the normal way of life in the 22nd century.
Children is playing in a “virtual forest”.
Are these images our future dreamed life?

error in my memory

This is the normal way of life in the 22nd century.
I reviewed my lifemoment archive of all seconds and found it failed to save one second of my life.
What will be the difference between our “memories’’ and “records‘‘?

simultaneous multiple city travel

This is the normal way of life in the 22nd century.
Most of the time we spend in a virtual world and we begin not to rely on our physical existences.
Can we discard our physical sense completely?

super moon 2115

This is the normal way of life in the 22nd century.
Today is the Super moon for the first time in 100 years!
How many things will remain after 100 years?

the end of the photography

This is the normal way of life in the 22nd century.
Finally, new media displaced photography and I decided to shoot my robotic wife in the final day of the photography.
What do you want to shoot in your last moment of the photography?

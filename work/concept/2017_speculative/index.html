Speculative Thinking

2017, Design Process Study
Private Work
Personal research of the experience design process in the 21st Century

Speculative Fusion of Design and Science

This is my own thought of the speculative fusion of design and science. In the business field, we have been creating probable futures using industrial solutions. Currently, through a collaboration with designers, technologists and business strategists, we can imagine and develop further plausible futures based on emerging technologies. Additionally, we must speculate possible futures that research-level technologies promise, and discuss the cultural and political mechanisms behind them to overcome our 21st-century problems. This is precisely the area in which I wish to study. In doing so, I aim to facilitate in-depth understanding of the implication of emerging technologies, and so help society make decisions regarding its collective future.

Speculative Design Process

This is my own research of design process. Based on my experience, learnings and talking with speculative designers, I visualized the 1st version of speculative design process. It is beyond design thinking to overcome our 21st-century problems. I want to explore this new practice and methods in my life. I have recently developed a great deal of appreciation for speculative design. Rather than setting out to solve problems, I look to use design as a medium to stimulate discussions about our future world. In synthesizing the potential of speculative design, I have been inspired by a lecture I attended by Hiromi Ozaki, also known as Sputniko!, former assistant professor of the design fiction group of the MIT Media Lab.

C-EPCO

2017, New Business Development | Professiona Work

Design Question:

How should the role of an energy company change with focusing on a customer experience?

In 2016, the deregulation of the electric power industry in Japan started on a full scale and a lot of emerging companies have been entering into the electtricity market. Chubu Electric Power Company (C-EPCO) must shift to a new paradigm of customer service to improve their retention capability.

Comprehensive Service Ecosystem Design

We have been cultivating a new paradigm of customer service that tightens family bonds using iPhone, Apple Watch and IoT Robot.

We are developing an iOS app with a robot toy that can communicate with children. By recording their daily responses and sharing these with the children’s parents and grandparents, the family members are able to understand the children’s hidden preferences and feel as if they are together, even if they live far apart.

As a project manager and professional UX designer, I contributed to the project from the concept phase to the agile development phase. In terms of technology, customer experience and sustainable business model, we have been designing entire new business ecosystem of the client.

In the concept phase, I planned an Design Thinking workshop with clients, created the initial concept for a new service, then narrowed this down to a series of actionable steps in consideration of the concept’s implementation. Additionally, in the subsequent development phase, I have been working on continuously improving the quality of the product through a process of iterative prototyping and user evaluation. I have the certification of both a practitioner of IBM Design Thinking and a Scrum Master, so I can work consistently end to end from the concept creation to implementation phases as a professional user experience designer.

Service Design is Co-creation

Product design is a process of exploring an appropriate ecosystem comprising everyone, that is created by everyone, and is provided for everyone.
My guiding principle is co-creation with clients, users, stakeholders, and experts to advance the journey between ideal concept and viable function; innovation and cliché; and provider’s aspiration and the customer’s need.
